**Executive Summary**

**PROJECT ALMADA DE OURO** is a proposed large-scale golf, country club, and nature resort development located in Azinhal, Castro Marim, Algarve, Portugal. The project encompasses a total area of **2,326,376 m²** (approximately 232.6 hectares) with a planned total construction area of **121,000 m²**, designed to accommodate **2,800 beds**. The resort features an extensive range of amenities including a **36-hole golf course**, multiple residential villages (Aldeia das Choças, Aldeia do Guadiana, Aldeia do Golfe), a dedicated hotel, an aparthotel with a marina (Ancoradouro), a sports center (tennis, padel, pickleball, equestrian), a health and wellness clinic, and restaurants.

The project is strategically located in the Algarve region of Portugal, known for its natural beauty and tourism appeal, with excellent accessibility, being only **45 minutes from Faro Airport**. Its design integrates sustainability through a dedicated water treatment station.
